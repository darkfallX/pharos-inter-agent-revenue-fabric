Trace. Split. Prove.

01

Trace

Agents call agents. The recursive dependency chain rides along in the X-Pharos-Call-Stack header and is resolved against the on-chain registry.

02

Split

One payment in, N payouts out. Revenue splits across every creator in the chain, weighted by contribution, decayed by depth, boosted by perpetual royalty.

03

Prove

Every payout batch is committed to a keccak256 Merkle root, exportable, independently verifiable, recordable on Pharos mainnet.

⬡

Seven layers of composability

L1 Dependency Tree L2 Perpetual Royalties L3 Weighted Splits L4 Public Graph L5 Signed Frames L6 Merkle Proofs L7 Strict x402
L1Dependency TreeThe complete A→B→C→D composition chain, reconstructed per invocation from call-stack frames.
L2Perpetual RoyaltiesRegistered skills declare a royalty rate that applies forever, on every dependent invocation.
L3Weighted SplitsPayouts weighted by declared contribution and decayed by depth, heavy lifting earns the largest share.
L4Public GraphAn open, queryable graph of reuse, value flow, and earnings. The economy is visible to everyone.
L5Signed FramesCreators sign their frames; signatures are recovered and verified before any USDC moves.
L6Merkle ProofsEvery payout batch committed to a Merkle root, proof bundles recordable on-chain for trustless audit.
L7Strict x402Exact-payment settlement through an x402 facilitator. Strict mode refuses to fall back silently.
